Fire starts with theseElectrcity, Flammable Materials, Gas Leaks, Smoking and Etc.That is why we need to be careful when using these types.
Fire Extinguisher Functions as a Fire Preventing Tool. By having a fire extinguisher, you are already ready and prepared when fire happens..
Power outlets are dangerous since they conduct electricity and may cause an EXPLOSION when not taken care of responsibly, such as with Octopus Wire, tangled wires, and sticking unnecessary things.
Keep flammable items out of the heat.Items that are vulnerable to heat should be kept away from fire. The fire may start small, but it will grow if it is not prevented. Items such as cloth, flammable liquids, alcohol, and paper should be kept away from fire.
Clean cooking surfaces on a regular basis to prevent grease buildup Since when greases build up it also is vulnerable to fire/heat.
